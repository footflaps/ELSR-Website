2024 ELSR Team Kit

We have brand new 2024 team kit available to order! If you want to try sample kit to find your size, we have a sample session in the Calendar Sample Session. Failing that, you can see the size charts here: Bioracer size charts.

To order, use This Link and register with Bioracer, who will then send a link to your email address. Once you have confirmed your email address, you will be able to access the site and order kit. You order and pay via the Bioracer website direct.

Once complete, the entire order ships to our Quartermaster (Graham) who will then distribute it to everyone. This method means no shipping fees.

Order deadline is Sunday 20th October!
