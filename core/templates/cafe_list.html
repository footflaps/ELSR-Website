ELSR Café Database – Cycle-Friendly Cafés Around Cambridge

We maintain an ever-growing database of cycle-friendly cafés in and around Cambridge, UK. These are the coffee stops and cafés our ride leaders regularly choose for weekend and mid-week group rides. Click a café on the map to view its description, facilities, and the cycling routes that pass through it.

The list is updated and curated by the ELSR community. If you know a café that deserves to be added, simply click the ADD CAFE button below and help other Cambridge riders discover new stops.

If you want to see the most popular cafes, from our group rides, click here: Top 10.

If you're thinking of planning a ride to a cafe and want some advice on how to do that, click here: How to choose a suitable route

Note: Cafés marked with a red location icon are temporarily or permanently closed.
