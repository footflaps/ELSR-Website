Chain what?

Every Thursday night, during the summer months, the best of the best meet on a Thursday night for a fight to the death chaingang where they attempt to rip each others legs off in a paceline. Imagine Max Max on bicycles crossed with Highlander and you're getting pretty close, although technically eye gouging is no longer permitted.

Fancy your chances?

Depending on numbers, there will be several groups based on ability, weight class and fighting style. This way, everyone stands a fair chance and can have a decent session with people of similar ability. Weigh-in commences 15 minutes before the start.

Where?

The meeting point is the Three Horseshoes pub in Madingley. The map, below, shows the 2023 course and the meeting point.
